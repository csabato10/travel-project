Get to Know Charlotte

  • Nicknamed the Queen City because of its namesake Charlotte of Mecklenburg-Strelitz.
  • Houses the University of North Carolina at Charlotte.
  • Artistic district called NoDa, great for a night out.
  • Uptown Charlotte has amazing skyscrapes and rooftop patios.
  • Pimento cheese captial of the World!
  • Home to the basketball team, the Charlotte Hornets.
  • Hometown of many famous people like Stephen Curry, Seth Curry, and DaBaby.
