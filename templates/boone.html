Get to know Boone

  • Boone is located in the Blue Ridge Mountains of west North Carolina.
  • Houses Appalachian State University.
  • Close by Grandfather Mountain, known for its great landscape.
  • Rent a cabin and relax!
  • The town is named after Daniel Boone, an American pioneer.
