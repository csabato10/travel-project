Get to know Miami

  • Miami is surrounded by two national parks: Biscayne National Park and Everglades National Park.
  • The population of Miami is 70% Hispanic.
  • There are over 50 shipwrecks off the coast of Miami. Great for a scuba diving.
  • Miami is immersed in culture, espeically Latin American cutlure.
  • Make sure to try Cuban food while you are there!
