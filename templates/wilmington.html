Get to know Wilmington

  • Seen in many shows and movies such as Dawson's Creek, One Tree Hill, Sleepy Hallow, etc.
  • There is a One Tree Hill convention every year, Chad Micheal Murray has even attended before.
  • Lively and clean beaches.
  • Houses the University of North Carolina at Wilmington.
  • Nicknamed the "Port City"
  • National Geographic named Wilmington one of the top 20 surf towns in the world. Come surf here!!
  • Woodrow Wilson was once a resident of Wilmington.
