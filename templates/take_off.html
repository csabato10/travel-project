Location is everything. Well, everything except budget, right? When most people think of a getaway, they imagine sitting on the beach coconut in hand, sun boiling their skin. When selecting from this list, choose your preference in location. Would you rather be in the thick of the wilderness? Exploring a city? Or enjoying that coconut?
