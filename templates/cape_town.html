Get to know Cape Town

  • Nicknamed the "Mother City".
  • Afrikaans is the most spoken langauge.
  • Appointed the best place in the world to visit by the New York Times in 2014.
  • One of the three capital cities of South Africa.
  • Known for its harbors and beaches, but also for Table Mountain.
  • Has a warm Mediterranean Climate.
  • Best time to visit is from March to May and September to November to avoid crowds.
